*SNAKE GAME INSTRUCTION*
OVERALL:
Your mission: Guide the snake eats the apple to grow longer.
Avoid the wall and your own tail.
The higher the level, the higher your score.

CONTROLS:
Use the arrow keys(Up, Down, Left, Right) to control.
The snake will move in the chosen direction.

GAMEPLAY:
Eating Apple: Move the snake to eat apple and grow longer.
Scoring: Every apple eaten adds to your score. 
Stop: Press the Space key to pause the game and continue.
Exit game: Use the X key to exit the game

GAME OVER:
The game ends if:
The snake collides with the walls.
The snake collides with its own tail.
The snake collides with the barrier(in high level).
